# Product requirements

## Goal

Enable neuroinclusive adults in Berlin to explore dating, friendship, and event connections through explicit preferences, bounded explanations, finite discovery, accessible controls, and accountable safety operations.

## Required participant capabilities before beta

- Authenticated invitation and adult confirmation.
- Separate connection modes and mutual eligibility controls.
- Field-level disclosure and matching-use choices, with neurotype matching off by default.
- Diagnosis absent from service/profile/matching surfaces.
- Multidimensional explanations that show alignment, difference and unknowns without a compatibility percentage.
- Finite candidate windows, pause, refresh schedule, and no penalty for inactivity.
- Conversation membership authorization, block, report, decision reasons and appeal.
- Consent, notice history, export, deletion, retention and contact/support routes.
- Keyboard, screen-reader, zoom/reflow, contrast, motion, error-recovery and cognitive-accessibility verification against WCAG 2.2.

## Product quality measures

Measure comprehension, control, accessibility, safety resolution, unwanted disclosure, distribution/exposure, unknown-field behavior, and participant-reported usefulness. Do not use time-in-app, message volume, reply speed, streaks, or return frequency as success proxies.

## Release boundary

The current local v1 is not a participant beta. Acceptance is controlled by `docs/control_room/RELEASE_GATES.md`.
