# Accessibility standard

Target: [WCAG 2.2 AA](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/quickref/) plus participatory cognitive and sensory testing. No conformance claim is permitted until a complete audit and remediation record exists.

Required before beta:

- Complete keyboard navigation, visible focus, meaningful headings/landmarks, labels, names/roles/states, and status/error announcements.
- 200% text resize, 400% reflow, no essential horizontal scrolling, sufficient contrast, non-color-only meaning, and reduced-motion support.
- Plain, direct copy; one primary action per step; progress and save state; review before irreversible actions; errors that preserve entered data.
- No forced time limits; predictable navigation; optional explanations/examples; control over animation and notification load.
- Accessible matching reasons, unknown states, visibility/use controls, report/block, consent, withdrawal, export and deletion.
- Screen-reader, keyboard-only, zoom, contrast, mobile viewport and cognitive walkthrough evidence with disabled/neurodivergent participants under an approved research protocol.

Automated checks supplement but do not replace manual and participant testing.
