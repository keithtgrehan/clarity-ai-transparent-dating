# Notification policy

Notifications are not implemented in the current MVP.

Future notifications must be off or minimal by default, purpose-labelled, quiet-hours aware, frequency-capped, screen-reader clear, and independently controllable for service, messages, events, safety, research, and marketing. Safety notices may override ordinary quiet settings only for a documented immediate-protection reason.

Prohibited copy includes urgency without an actual deadline, guilt about inactivity, popularity/social-proof pressure, streak loss, reply-speed judgment, and ambiguous “someone is waiting” prompts. Every non-essential notification must have a direct unsubscribe/control path and no adverse matching effect.
