# Research protocol gate

No participant recruitment, interviews, usability sessions, event pilots, private-data collection, annotation, or model training is authorized by this document.

Before research starts, approve:

- research question, necessity, method, inclusion/exclusion and Berlin/18+ safeguards;
- controller/lawful-basis/Article 9/DPIA analysis and participant notice;
- separate research and any diagnosis/NLP-feedback consent, comprehension check, withdrawal and deletion path;
- recruitment language, power/coercion review, compensation, accessibility and safeguarding;
- minimised instruments, pseudonymization, access roles, processors, region/transfer, retention, deletion and incident procedure;
- distress, disclosure and underage escalation without presenting Clarity as crisis or clinical support;
- publication/quotation/recontact choices and a prohibition on silent service-to-training reuse.

Research outputs must separate observation, participant quotation permissions, interpretation, uncertainty, adverse findings, and design decisions. Findings do not justify subgroup generalization without adequate evidence.
