# Research index

Last reviewed: 2026-07-15

Status: canonical bibliography and source-status map for a synthetic local research milestone. This is not an exhaustive systematic review, legal advice, participant-research approval, corpus authorization, or evidence of product effectiveness.

## Coverage and method

The July 2026 pass used 20 focused discovery queries across autism and ADHD romantic relationships, AuDHD evidence gaps, reciprocal communication, partner responsiveness, repair, prosody, dialect/code-switching, text anonymisation, synthetic data, recommender fairness and anti-compulsion design. Firecrawl search was used for discovery; claims were checked against publisher, DOI, PubMed/PMC, ACM, ACL Anthology, Nature, PLOS or official pages where available. The pass reviewed more than 25 primary pages and retained 45 peer-reviewed publications, 10 official/legal/standards/platform-policy sources, four community-resource classes, and 11 exact committed legacy research documents.

No Reddit post, YouTube comment, forum thread, support-group contribution, username, diagnosis, quote or raw page was scraped into the repository, retained as research data, or converted into a fixture or label. Search-result discovery of community source classes was metadata-only. Source rights and product-training decisions are governed by `configs/training_source_registry.yml`.

| Status | Meaning |
