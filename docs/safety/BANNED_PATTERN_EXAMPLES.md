# Banned Pattern Examples

## Purpose
Examples are here to support moderation guidance and simple pattern-based review. They are illustrative, not exhaustive.

## Manipulative Pressure
- “If you cared, you would reply right now.”
- “You owe me a response.”
- “Do this now or I’m done.”

## Unsafe Off-Platform Pressure
- “Come to my hotel tonight.”
- “Send me your exact address first.”
- “Move to private chat immediately.”

## Scam / Extraction
- “Can you send me money first?”
- “I need a transfer before we meet.”

## Fetishization
- treating neurodivergence as novelty, roleplay, or education service
- asking someone to “prove” their neurotype for curiosity or fantasy

## Notes
- examples should be expanded with real moderation learnings later
- pattern matching should always feed review, not replace it
