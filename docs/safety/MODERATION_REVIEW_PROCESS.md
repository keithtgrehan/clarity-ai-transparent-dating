# Moderation Review Process

Status: future gated operating procedure only. No review queue, moderator console, access-control boundary, decision record, appeal workflow, or staffed operation exists in v1. The authoritative current-state policy is `docs/safety/moderation_policy.md`.

## First-Pass Workflow
1. a future authenticated report or approved bounded-assistance flag enters an access-controlled review queue
2. an authorized human reviewer examines only the minimum permitted evidence
3. the reviewer classifies the issue and records a reasoned decision
4. immediate user block protection remains separate from platform sanctions

## Review Questions
- what specific behavior crossed a platform boundary
- is there enough evidence to act
- does this require immediate hold or just follow-up review
- is the issue harassment, coercion, scam behavior, or another clear category

## Current Boundaries
- no fully automated permanent bans
- no clinical interpretation
- no diagnosis authenticity judgment

## Missing Operational Pieces
- moderator dashboard
- appeals workflow
- response-time SLA
- escalation rules for repeated offenders
