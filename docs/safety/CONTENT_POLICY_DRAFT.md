# Content Policy Draft

## Scope
This is a first-pass platform safety draft for a dating product. It is not legal advice and needs formal review before any public launch.

## Allowed
- respectful dating intent
- direct expression of communication needs
- discussion of sensory preferences and boundaries
- consensual flirting that remains respectful and non-coercive

## Not Allowed
- harassment or repeated unwanted contact
- boundary violations
- coercion or manipulative pressure
- hate, demeaning language, or bigotry
- impersonation or deceptive identity claims
- fetishization of neurodivergence
- sexual content without clear consent
- scams, money requests, or pressure to move off-platform unsafely

## Enforcement Direction
- block/report access should always be available
- high-risk categories should route to human review
- AI may suggest categories but should not be final authority

## Legal Review Needed
- final policy language
- appeal workflow
- age-related policy
- evidence retention and export rules
