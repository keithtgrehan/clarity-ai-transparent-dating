# Profile Signal Taxonomy

## High-Signal Traits
- explicit relationship intent
- clear communication preferences
- concrete sensory or date-format notes
- specific first-date examples
- useful interests and Berlin logistics

## Low-Signal Traits
- filler phrases
- minimal bio
- no communication guidance
- no usable first-date context

## Caution Signals
- contradictions between structured answers and text
- unclear intent language
- repeated placeholder responses
