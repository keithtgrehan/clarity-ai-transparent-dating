# Roadmap

> Historical roadmap retained for context. The authoritative dependency-ordered roadmap and release conditions are `docs/control_room/MASTER_PLAN.md`, `BACKLOG.md`, and `RELEASE_GATES.md`.

## Horizon
This roadmap assumes a founder build cadence of roughly 30 hours per week.

## Phase 0: First-Pass Foundation
Status: in progress in this repo run

Goals:
- define product thesis, launch scope, safety position, and architecture
- stand up local monorepo
- create domain models, routes, and calm UI scaffolds
- create Berlin-first waitlist and seed fixtures

## Phase 1: Usable Local Prototype
Target: next 2 to 3 weeks

Deliverables:
- auth decision and session strategy
- improved onboarding completion flow
- editable candidate filters
- better conversation threading
- founder moderation dashboard stub
- stronger fixture coverage and route tests

## Phase 2: Founder-Led Research Loop
Target: weeks 3 to 6

Deliverables:
- interview guide and structured notes
- waitlist segmentation review
- landing-page copy iterations
- candidate explanation tuning
- moderation playbook for pilot invite stage

## Phase 3: Berlin Pilot Readiness
Target: weeks 6 to 10

Deliverables:
- real privacy, consent, and retention review
- account lifecycle baseline
- admin review and block tooling
- profile quality thresholds
- manual invite and hold states

## Phase 4: Early Pilot Operations
Target: after readiness gates pass

Deliverables:
- controlled Berlin-only invitations
- manual moderation response workflow
- explicit analytics review
- weekly product and safety retrospective

## Readiness Gates Before Public Pilot
- profile quality rubric exists
- safety reporting reviewed
- deletion/export/legal TODOs narrowed into concrete work
- enough candidate density in Berlin neighborhoods or circles
- founder operations burden remains sustainable

## Things To Refuse Until Later
- dopamine-loop engagement features
- “AI matchmaking magic” marketing
- geographic expansion before density
- onboarding bloat
- generalized social network features
