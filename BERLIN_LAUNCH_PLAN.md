# Berlin Launch Plan

> Historical discovery draft, not an authorization to recruit, collect data, or launch. `docs/control_room/MASTER_PLAN.md`, `docs/research/research_protocol.md`, and Gates G4–G5 supersede its dates, cohort sizes, metrics, and readiness checklist.

## Launch Goal
Validate whether a Berlin-first neurodivergent dating product can attract enough high-intent early users to support a safe, structured pilot rather than a passive waitlist.

## Why Berlin First
- strong concentration of ADHD, autistic, and AuDHD adults across tech, creative, academic, and international communities
- relatively high openness to alternative product experiences
- compact geography for interviews, pilot events, and trust-building
- easier founder learning loops than starting broad and remote

## Geographic Boundaries For Launch
Primary focus:
- Berlin city users only

Secondary support for early waitlist intake:
- Potsdam and nearby commuters can join the waitlist, but should be tagged separately

Do not launch nationally in this phase. Density and trust matter more than total signups.

## First 3 Launch Cohorts

### Cohort 1: founder-recruited signal group
- 20 to 40 people
- sourced from personal network, warm intros, Berlin ND communities, and trusted operators
- goal: profile language quality, onboarding friction, safety expectations

### Cohort 2: interview-to-waitlist group
- 75 to 150 people
- sourced from niche communities, newsletters, event circles, and creators
- goal: refine messaging, identify strongest hooks and biggest trust blockers

### Cohort 3: pilot matching pool
- 50 to 100 active profiles after quality filtering
- goal: test compatibility model assumptions, messaging norms, and moderation workload

## Launch Messaging
Lead with:
- less ambiguity
- more explicit compatibility
- calmer dating experience
- structured communication preferences

Avoid leading with:
- medicalized language
- “dating for broken people” framing
- vague wellness or self-help positioning

## Acquisition Channels To Test
- Berlin neurodivergent creator partnerships
- local newsletters and event communities
- founder-led outreach to ADHD/autism/AuDHD community spaces
- Reddit and Discord only if tone and moderation fit the product values
- direct interview recruitment through waitlist form

## Local Research Loop
Every week during pre-launch:
1. run 3 to 5 user conversations
2. classify objections, trust blockers, and copy confusion
3. update onboarding prompts and landing copy
4. review whether safety burden is growing faster than product clarity

## Operational Guardrails
- do not chase top-of-funnel volume before moderation and profile quality thresholds exist
- do not allow city-wide growth without a clear block/report workflow
- do not overpromise AI assistance or compatibility accuracy
- do not open to neurotypical audiences until filtering, expectations, and safety norms are explicit

## Pre-Launch Readiness Checklist
- waitlist schema captures enough segmentation to recruit useful testers
- profile model supports Berlin-specific context such as language comfort and transit radius
- moderation categories exist
- deletion/export/legal TODOs are documented
- founder can manually review a small pilot without burnout

## Key Metrics For Berlin First Pass
- waitlist conversion from landing page
- percent of signups willing to do interviews
- onboarding completion rate in local testing
- percent of profiles considered “high-signal”
- match candidate acceptance in manual review
- report rate per active conversation

## Practical Launch Sequence
Week 1:
- founder interviews and landing-page feedback

Week 2:
- waitlist capture live to trusted communities

Week 3:
- manually invite highest-signal Berlin users into profile/onboarding pilot

Week 4:
- test candidate matching and first conversations with close monitoring

## Hard Questions To Answer Before Real Launch
- what minimum profile quality is required before entering the matching pool
- what languages are supported at launch beyond English and German
- how much manual moderation can be sustained weekly
- whether date-intent and friendship-intent should stay combined or split early
