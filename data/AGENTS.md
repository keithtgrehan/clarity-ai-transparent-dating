# Data directory instructions

Only synthetic local-development data is allowed in this directory.

Prohibited content includes:

- real or plausibly real participant profiles, emails, messages, reports, diagnosis information, or consent records;
- raw transcripts, private research notes, unreviewed exports, provider responses, or annotation-tool dumps;
- credentials, tokens, cookies, production identifiers, or database copies;
- downloaded datasets or model artifacts without an approved phase, licence record, and retention owner.

Synthetic fixtures must use unmistakably fictional identities, document their purpose, and avoid realistic contact details except reserved domains such as `example.test`. Mutable runtime data stays ignored under `data/runtime`.
